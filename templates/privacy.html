Privacy Policy

When you register and use iFund Marketing, we may collect the following information:

  • Withdrawal details (payment method and account information)

We only collect information that is necessary for platform functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

Collected information is used to:

We do not sell or rent user data to third parties.

3. Payment & Withdrawal Information

Withdrawal details such as GCash or bank account information are collected solely for payout processing purposes.

These details are not shared with unauthorized parties and are handled with strict confidentiality.
