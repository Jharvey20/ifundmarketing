About iFund Marketing

What is iFund Marketing?

iFund Marketing is a beta-stage online earning platform that allows members to earn rewards by completing simple tasks and participating in referral-based promotions. The platform is designed with sustainability, transparency, and controlled growth in mind.

iFund Marketing operates on a limited-access system to ensure that payouts remain manageable and fair for all members.

Ownership & Management

iFund Marketing is currently operated and managed by an independent Filipino founder as a small-scale digital project under development.

As a beta platform, iFund Marketing is not yet registered as a corporation. Formal registration (DTI) will be completed once the platform reaches a stable operational phase.

All operational decisions, system rules, and payout management are handled directly by the platform owner to ensure accountability and stability.

How the Platform Works

  • Users activate their account using a valid activation code.
  • Members earn points by completing available tasks.
  • Points can be converted into cash balance.
  • Cash balance may be withdrawn once withdrawal conditions are met.

The system limits the number of activations and payouts to protect the platform from abuse and financial instability.

Activation & Entry Fee

  • Account activation requires a valid activation code.
  • Activation codes are priced at ₱190.
  • Activation slots are limited.

Activation fees are used to fund platform operations, payouts, and system maintenance.

Referral Program

  • Members earn ₱50 for every successful referral.
  • Referral rewards are credited after successful account activation.
  • Referral earnings are added directly to the member’s cash balance.

Referral rewards are subject to adjustment during the beta phase.

Withdrawals & Payouts

  • Minimum withdrawal amount: ₱300
  • Processing fee: 5%
  • Net payout for ₱300 withdrawal: ₱285
  • Withdrawals are processed weekly in batches.
  • Processing time: 1–2 business days after approval.

Withdrawal availability may require a waiting period depending on account status and platform conditions.

To ensure platform stability, only a limited number of withdrawals are processed per week.

Fees & Charges Summary

  • Activation fee: ₱190
  • Referral commission: ₱50
  • Withdrawal processing fee: 5%

No hidden fees are charged beyond those stated above.

Risk Disclosure

iFund Marketing does not guarantee fixed income or instant payouts. Earnings depend on task availability, system performance, and platform sustainability.

Members are encouraged to participate responsibly and understand that this is a beta platform under continuous development.

Beta Disclaimer

iFund Marketing is currently in BETA.
Features, pricing, rewards, and rules may be modified at any time to ensure long-term sustainability and system integrity.

By using the platform, members acknowledge and accept these conditions.

Contact & Support

Support is currently handled through in-platform assistance and official communication channels.

For urgent concerns, users are advised to follow platform announcements.
